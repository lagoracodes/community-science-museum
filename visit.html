The entrance is free for all.
There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.
If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The Community Science Museum is dedicated to ensuring that all visitors can access the museum fully, and measures are taken to guarantee that visitors can easily locate all the necessary resources in the exhibition rooms and at every stage of their visit. The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drinks

The museum has a nice café where you can enjoy a variety of food and drinks. From light lunches and snacks to coffee and soft drinks, there's something for everyone. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a relaxed meal, the café adds to your museum experience, providing a cozy spot to recharge and enjoy some tasty treats.

Shop

Visit our shop to find museum souvenirs and gifts that let you carry the excitement of science with you. Explore a range of memorabilia and unique items inspired by the exhibits. Plus, discover activity packs that bring science to life even after your museum visit. Take home a piece of the museum and keep the curiosity alive!
