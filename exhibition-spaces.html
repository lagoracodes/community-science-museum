Exhibition spaces

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, 'The Sky Above Us', explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We'll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by. Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo's telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system. At our museum, 'The Sky Above Us' is not just an exhibition; it's a journey through time and space that inspires wonder and curiosity. We invite you to join us in this cosmic adventure and experience the thrill of discovery that has captured the imagination of generations.

Evolution

For centuries, philosophers and scientists have wrestled with the question of our origins. Where do we come from and how did we get here? Since Darwin proposed his theory of evolution we have had a framework for exploring and understanding our place in this world. Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved in our time. You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn't survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs.

Biology and Medicine

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology offer us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world. For many people, their life expectancy is much longer and their quality of life much improved, thanks to the growth of our understanding of medicine. Over a series of exhibits, we explore the history of medicine and take a look at some major breakthroughs including the discovery of penicillin and the first heart transplant. Many of our great medicinal discoveries have come not only from the lab, but also from observing animals in the wild. Often our fellow creatures have beaten us to it.

Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot. As we continue to explore the vast potential of AI and robotics, we invite you to be a part of this transformative journey. The information revolution is not just on the horizon; it's here, and our museum is your gateway to understanding, interacting with, and celebrating the science of the future.

Ecology

As we learn more and more about the impact humans are having on the planet, the more important it becomes that scientists explore how to create a sustainable future not just for humans but for the whole planet. In our ecology section of the museum, we unpack the dangers we currently face including global warming, extinction of species, and pollution. But we also focus on the positive ways we can all contribute to a brighter future. As you explore our ecology section, you'll not only come to appreciate the intricate beauty of our planet but also discover how individual and collective efforts can make a real difference. Together, we can forge a path towards a more sustainable and harmonious future for both humanity and the diverse life forms with which we share this precious planet.
